the rest (of N)¶

  • 剩餘(的…)
  • N可能為可數或不可數，可數時可能單數也可能複數，依前後文判斷
  • There are some Taiwanese students in the class; the rest (of the students) are Americans.
    = There are some Taiwanese students in the class, the rest of whom are Americans.
    = There are some Taiwanese students in the class. The rest (of them) are Americans.
    = There are some Taiwanese students in the class, and the rest (of them) are Americans.

…no one/nobody…anymore & …not…anymore¶

...no one/nobody...anymore再也沒有人= ...no one/nobody...any longer
...not...anymore再也沒有 ; 不再= ...not...any longer= ...no longer
