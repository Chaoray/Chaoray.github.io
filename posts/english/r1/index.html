valuable¶

valuable adj. 寶貴的 反義：worthless (adj.) 無價值的 ; 不值錢的

schedule¶

文法¶

V-ing + V-es…¶

  • 動名詞當主詞 -> 第三人稱單數

grab/catch/… + sb. + prep. + the + body part¶

grab sb. by the arm = grab one's arm 抓某人的手臂
catch sb. by the wrist/elbow = catch one's arm 抓某人的手腕/手肘
take sb. by the arm/hand = take one's arm/hand 抓某人的手臂/牽某人的手
hit sb. in the stomach/face = hit one's stomach/face 打某人的肚子/臉
kiss sb. on the cheek/lips = kiss one's cheek/lips 親某人的臉頰/雙脣
pat sb. on the shoulder = pat one's shoulder 拍某人的肩

In order to/To + VR, S + V…¶

為了要... 為了不要...
(in order) to in order not to (in order不能省) 可以放句中、句首
so as to so as not to 可以放句中，不能放句首
