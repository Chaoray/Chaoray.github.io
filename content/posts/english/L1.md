## the rest (of N)
- 剩餘(的...)
- N可能為可數或不可數，可數時可能單數也可能複數，依前後文判斷
- There are some Taiwanese students in the class<u>; the rest (of the students)</u> are Americans.</br>= There are some Taiwanese students in the class<u>, the rest of <red>whom</red></u> are Americans.</br>= There are some Taiwanese students in the class<u>. The rest (of <red>them</red>)</u> are Americans.</br>= There are some Taiwanese students in the class<u>, and the rest (of <red>them</red>)</u> are Americans.
