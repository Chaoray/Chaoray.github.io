## due to
- 因為
- 同義
  - because of
  - owing to
  - on owing of

## all of a sudden
- 突然間
- 同義
  - all at once
  - suddenly

## turn out
- 結果是 ; 最終成為
- turn out to be adj/N
- As it turns out, S + V...
- It turns out that S + V...

## come across
- 遇到 ; 偶然碰見
- 同義
  - run across
  - run into
  - bump into
  - encounter

## carry out
- 執行 ; 進行
- carry out
  - research 進行研究
  - an investigation 進行調查
  - an experiment 做實驗
